My Tech Journey At She Code Africa

About Me

My name is Nafisat Akokhia, and I am an aspiring software engineer specializing in frontend development. I am passionate about creating visually appealing user interfaces and i have background knowledge in HTML, CSS, TailwindCSS, JavaScript, and React.js. I have also worked on various projects using these technologies, building functional and responsive websites that enhance user experience.

My Tech Journey

My tech journey began in early 2024 when I decided to transition from a non-tech field into frontend development. I made the bold decision to resign from my previous job and fully dedicate myself to learning. I enrolled in a 3-month bootcamp where I learned foundational skills in HTML, CSS, TailwindCSS, and JavaScript. After completing the bootcamp, I continued my learning journey independently, diving deeper into HTML, CSS, TailwindCSS, JavaScript, and React.js through resources like YouTube tutorials and official documentation.

In November 2024, my growth accelerated when I had the incredible opportunity to join a program supported by the Lagos State Government, Glovo, and She Code Africa. This program has been instrumental in shaping my skills, and my learning experience has been nothing short of fantastic. At She Code Africa, Ive been fortunate to learn from dedicated facilitator like Mr. Emmanuel Adeyemo, who ensures every concept is clearly explained and encourages us to ask questions without hesitation. His guidance has been both motivating and insightful. Also to my P.O.C (Point Of Contact) Miss / Mrs Trust Ada, is another inspiring figure consistently reminding us to persevere and stay focused. Her attention to detail and support has made the program even more rewarding. I'm immensely grateful to the Lagos State Government, Glovo, and She Code Africa for this invaluable opportunity to grow and thrive in the tech field.

My Goals

In three years from now i see myself becoming a fullstack developer, having solid foundation in both frontend and backend technologies, and also work on real-world projects that solve meaningful problems.
